Power Platform

Intelligent apps and automation for BC organizations

PowerApps, Power Automate, Power Pages, and Dataverse — Microsoft's low-code platform built into your existing M365 environment, turning manual processes and disconnected data into intelligent, automated workflows without requiring a development team.

The four pillars

One platform. Four powerful tools.

Power Platform is not one product — it's an integrated suite that works best when all four components are built to work together. Here's what each delivers.

📱

PowerApps

CANVAS + MODEL-DRIVEN

Custom applications built in days, not months. Canvas apps for mobile field workers — case workers logging data from remote communities, health care workers recording wellness touchpoints, transit inspectors logging fleet conditions. Model-driven apps for structured data entry against your Dataverse tables. Both work offline and sync when connectivity is restored.

Canvas AppsModel-DrivenOffline CapableMobile First
⚡

Power Automate

WORKFLOW + INTEGRATION

Automated workflows that eliminate manual steps. Approval chains, scheduled report delivery via email or Teams, data synchronization between systems, triggered notifications when Power BI thresholds are breached, and robotic process automation (RPA) for legacy systems that don't have APIs. The difference between a dashboard that shows a problem and an organization that responds to it automatically.

ApprovalsRPATeams NotificationsScheduled Flows
🌐

Power Pages

EXTERNAL PORTALS

Secure external-facing portals without building a custom web application. Community member portals for First Nations health programs where members can view their own data, submit information, and track requests. Government client portals where procurement officers access project status and deliverables. All backed by Dataverse with role-based access control.

External AccessDataverse BackedRole-Based Security
🗄️

Dataverse

INTELLIGENT DATA LAYER

The data backbone that connects all four Power Platform components — and connects them to your Dynamics 365 environment if you have one. Structured, governed, audited data storage with built-in column-level security, audit history, and business rules. The difference between apps built on spreadsheets and apps built on an enterprise data platform.

Government and public sector applications

What Power Platform looks like for BC organizations

Inspection & Compliance

Replace paper forms and email attachments with a PowerApps inspection app that captures data offline, takes photos, generates compliance reports automatically, and routes findings through an approval workflow in Power Automate.

Grant & Program Management

External application portals via Power Pages, automated eligibility screening in Power Automate, status tracking for applicants, and reporting dashboards for program administrators — all connected in one Dataverse-backed system.

HR & Onboarding

Automated onboarding workflows, equipment request apps, policy acknowledgment tracking, and leave management systems that integrate with your existing M365 environment — without a custom software project.

Contract & Document Management

Contract lifecycle management built in PowerApps and Dataverse — creation, routing for approval, execution tracking, renewal alerts, and searchable document storage — replacing SharePoint lists and email chains.

Field Data Collection

Mobile apps for caseworkers, inspectors, health workers, and field teams who need to record structured data away from a desk. Offline-capable, photo-enabled, GPS-tagged, and synced to your central Dataverse tables automatically.

Dynamics 365 Integration

If your organization runs Dynamics 365 — Customer Service, Sales, or Government — we extend it with Power Platform components and connect it to your analytics layer for a unified data picture across operations and reporting.
