About Us

BC's specialist in public-sector data intelligence

Null&Void Analytics Inc. is a federally incorporated BC analytics consultancy founded on a simple premise: the organizations making the most consequential decisions in British Columbia deserve the same quality of data intelligence that the largest enterprises in the world have access to.

Our principal

23 years turning enterprise data problems into competitive advantages

The principal consultant at Null&Void Analytics brings over two decades of enterprise IT experience, with more than ten years specializing in the Microsoft data and analytics stack. That career spans large-scale infrastructure projects, enterprise application deployments, and increasingly complex data engineering and analytics engagements across healthcare, government, and the private sector.

The decision to found Null&Void Analytics came from a consistent observation across those years: BC's public sector organizations — health authorities, First Nations, transit agencies, justice ministries — were being underserved by large consulting firms that rotated generalist staff through government contracts, and by boutique shops that lacked the technical depth to deliver enterprise-grade solutions. There was a gap between what was being built and what was needed.

Null&Void exists to close that gap — with the technical capability of an enterprise practice and the accountability of a firm where the principal is on every engagement.

Why we exist

What we believe about data and the public sector

Data sovereignty is non-negotiable

For First Nations organizations, sovereignty over their own data is a right, not a preference. OCAP principles are not a compliance checkbox in our work — they are the starting point for every conversation and every technical design decision.

The principal is always in the room

Large consulting firms rotate junior staff through government contracts. At Null&Void, the same certified, experienced consultant who writes your proposal designs your architecture, builds your pipelines, and trains your staff. There is no bait-and-switch.

Privacy is designed in, not bolted on

A Privacy Impact Assessment is a deliverable in every healthcare and justice engagement — not an afterthought. We design data flows, access controls, and governance frameworks before we write a single line of pipeline code.

Your data stays in your tenant

Every engagement is delivered into the client's Azure or on-premise environment. Null&Void never hosts client data in our own subscription. You own everything — the data, the code, the documentation — at the end of every project.

Boutique speed, enterprise standards

A 12-week engagement that would take a large firm 40 weeks. The same governance, the same architecture standards, the same compliance documentation — delivered faster because there's no overhead, no account management layers, no committees.

Training is a deliverable, not an afterthought

Every project ends with training sessions for frontline staff, managers, and executives, and a full documentation package. The most common failure mode in analytics projects is dashboards nobody uses. We prevent that by making sure the people who need the data know how to use it.
